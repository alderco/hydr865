FAQ

What are the benefits of IV infusion?

Intravenous infusion, including injections, deliver fluids, vitamins, antioxidants, and nutrients directly into the bloodstream. Bypassing the gastrointestinal tract allows the body to absorb 100% of all the nutrients and vitamins allowing you to heal and recover quickly.

How long does it take?

To be most efficient, we request our intake forms be completed online before your appointment. Most sessions will take 45 - 60 minutes. We will offer an “express lunch” session averaging 30 minutes. Intramuscular injections take less than 5 minutes on average.

If pregnant, can I get an IV?

We do not administer vitamins to pregnant women. We will administer fluids to pregnant women looking to hydrate if a permission note is provided by their obstetrician.

If under 18, can I get an IV?

Anyone under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian (documentation is required) to receive IV fluids.

Committed to Safety

For your safety and ours, the following must be completed before an IV infusion session can start:

  1. Electronic medical questionnaire
  2. Taking of client vital signs
  3. Intake conversation and assessment with licensed staff
  4. Patient treatment agreement and authorization

Hydr865 does not offer diagnostic services and our guidelines are strict. Your health and medical history may disqualify you from our IV infusion or injection therapy. Hydr865 is unable to provide services to pregnant women and oncology patients without written consent (Dr.’s note) from your personal obstetrician or doctor.

For your safety, your chosen IV infusion “recipe” is mixed in a filtered hood vent to prevent any contaminants from entering the mixture. This process allows us to provide you with a reliable mixture of multiple vitamins and nutrients for your infusion, getting the most bang for your buck. We also do not pre-mix our infusions. When infusions are pre-mixed and sit on a shelf their full potency is lost. You are coming to us to feel better and we want you to leave feeling renewed, restored, and ready to reclaim your day.
